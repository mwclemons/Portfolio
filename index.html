About Me

I have been a Senior IT Business Analyst on the Portfolio Management Team at Essilor of America since 2011. In that time, I have been responsible for meeting with business resources to identify requirements for IT projects. I have worked on one the largest IT projects ever delivered by Essilor IT in the EyeMed Project, the UHC Project and the Versant Project. These three projects allowed Essilor to help these partners secure spots in the top 5 market share for eyecare benefits providers. Prior to transitioning into IT, I was a Senior Financial Analyst supporting the IT department at Essilor. In this role, I supported the CIO, IT leadership and the Lab Management System (LMS) leadership by coordinating the budgeting process to ensure budgeting and forecasting deadlines were met.

Before joining Essilor, I was a Financial Analyst at SourceCorp in Dallas. While at SourceCorp, Michael completed my MBA at Southern Methodist University while working fulltime.

Prior to SourceCorp, my first job after completing my undergraduate work at the California Institute of Technology was as a Risk Analyst at Hunt Financial Ventures in Dallas. As a Risk Analyst, I analyzed and monitored the risks of multi-strategy, convertible arbitrage, equity long-short, credit arbitrage, natural resources, special situations and statistical arbitrage hedge funds.

While my experience and time as an IT Business Analyst has been very rewarding and challenging at times, I am currently looking to move into a more technical role as a developer. To accomplish this, I took the initiative to complete a 6 month Full Stack Bootcamp at SMU while working fulltime. In this class I have developed my coding skills. I am in a unique position to use my new skills, my experience as BA, and my problem solving skills to add value to a company as a developer. I am looking forward to the next challenge in my career.
